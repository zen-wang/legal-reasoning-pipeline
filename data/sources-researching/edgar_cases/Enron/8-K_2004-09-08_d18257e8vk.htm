Section 1 — Registrant’s Business and Operations

Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

     On September 1, 2004, Enron Corp. (the “Company”) and its debtor-in-possession subsidiaries (collectively with the Company, the “Debtors”) entered into a Closing Agreement On Final Determination Covering Specific Matters with the United States Commissioner of Internal Revenue (the “Closing Agreement”). A copy of the Closing Agreement is attached as Exhibit 10.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.

     The Closing Agreement sets forth the dates upon which the Debtors are required to recognize discharge of indebtedness income for federal income tax purposes in connection with the discharge of various types of claims pursuant to their Supplemental Modified Fifth Amended Joint Plan of Affiliated Debtors Pursuant to Chapter 11 of the United States Bankruptcy Code (the “Chapter 11 Plan”). This description of the Closing Agreement is qualified by reference to the complete text of the Closing Agreement.
