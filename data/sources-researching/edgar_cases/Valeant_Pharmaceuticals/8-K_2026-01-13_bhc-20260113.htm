Item 2.02 Results of Operations and Financial Condition
As previously announced through a news wire release on January 5, 2026, Bausch Health Companies Inc. (the “Company” or “Bausch Health”) will participate in the 44th Annual J.P. Morgan Healthcare Conference (the “JPM Conference”) on January 14, 2026. The Company expects to present at the JPM Conference at approximately 3:45 p.m. PT (6:45 p.m. ET). During the presentation, the Company’s management will provide an update for investors relating to certain strategic and business updates. The Company’s management also expects to reaffirm the full year 2025 guidance at the higher end of the range for Bausch Health excluding Bausch + Lomb Corporation that it provided in its financial results press release for the third quarter of 2025 dated October 29, 2025.
A live audio webcast of the event will be accessible on the Investor Relations section of Bausch Health’s website (http://ir.bauschhealth.com/events-and-presentations). A copy of the presentation and a replay of the event will be available on the Company’s Investor Relations website following the event.
